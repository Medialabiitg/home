Narrative and Storytelling in the age of Virtual Reality


The 2-D screen is still the fundamental visual medium of today. The screen has adapted and
inculcated various properties such as interactivity etc. but the nature of content that could
be exchanged through screen as a medium is still monolithic in an experiential sense. One of
the various alternatives that has been proposed is Virtual Reality Medium. This project is an attempt to create an Avant-Garde film in Virtual Reality medium. As an experimental film, our project is about finding out more about the possibilities the medium has to offer and integrating them into our approach towards the medium.

“To explore the new dimensions being possible in Virtual Reality and gauging
how they can influence the experiential aspect of narrative by demonstrating
the developed concepts using an avant-garde vr film.”

Project by Mahendra Varma & Pradeep Vegireddi
