Study in Distributed system in Nature and Blockchain

This is creation of ‘nature’ of a system by observation of processes in plants. Here is the ‘Meta’ study which is study of observations and contemplation on biological studies. ‘Nature’ is whole and fragmented, from fragments can be created a behaviour as living systems in human-techno-social systems.

Design Innovation by

Nature System Design | Distributed Systems | Biology
Community | Consilience | Design Research | Post Humanism

Project by Abhishek Chauhan

Guided by Mriganka Madhukaillya


Blockchain is a technology that is capable of modeling human interaction as  natural living systems. Ethereum virtual machine with smart contracts is a tool to be explored in systemic designing like living systems. 

This study has rendered in creation of form and structure for system intended  to achieve ‘consilience’ or ‘unity of knowledge’. Taking plant organogenesis as a process of inspiration and studying genotype and phenotype of flower development alongwith the auxin flow for flower growth, analogus system is conceptualised with use of Smart contracts.
